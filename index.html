What is an ERP Software?

ERP stands for Enterprise Resource Planning — a type of software that unifies all core functions of a business into one streamlined system. Instead of juggling separate tools for accounting, inventory, HR, and sales, an ERP connects everything in real time. This helps teams collaborate better, reduces duplication, and gives decision-makers a clear overview of operations. .
