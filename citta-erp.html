What is an ERP Software?

ERP stands for Enterprise Resource Planning. It is a type of software that integrates all core functions of a business into one streamlined system. Rather than juggling separate tools for accounting, inventory, HR, and sales, ERP software connects everything in real time. It improves team collaboration, eliminates data duplication, and gives business leaders a clear, unified view of operations.
